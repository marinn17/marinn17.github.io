Upstairs (2019)

This video is a response to an assignment in my Intro to the Moving Image class that asked us to explore a location. I wanted to play with the idea of the mundane being turned into something more. Stairs, help us go from one place to another. I've noticed how sometimes getting from place to place, I get lost in my thoughts and walk on auto-pilot. As we walk through life a bit distracted, would we notice if something strange occurred?
