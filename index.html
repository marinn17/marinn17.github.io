Who Am I?

I'm currently a rising junior at Wellesley College studying Computer Science and Media Studies. I'm interested in the intersection between arts and technology. As our world increasingly becomes automated by machines, I think it is important that we don't lose touch with our humanity. Likewise, I don't think technology is something we should be afraid of but instead something that is helping us create a better future.

When I'm not working on class projects, I'm usually out on the Charles River. On campus I'm part of the varsity crew team, a member of the Davis Museum Student Advisory Committee and a layout editor for Counterpoint Magazine.
